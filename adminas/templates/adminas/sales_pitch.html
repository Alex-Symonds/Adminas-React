What is Adminas?

Adminas is an order entry program which administrators can use to enter a purchase order, check the prices and specifications are correct, then output an order confirmation and works order card as PDFs.

Adminas Features

Check prices on a purchase order against both the standard list price and any applicable resale discounts.

Easy organisation of modular items: assign components, accessories and ancillary items to their parent item.

Issue documents your way, distributing line items across as many order confirmations and works order cards as you need.

Public comments on each job keep everyone in the loop, while private comments allow users to add their own reminders and notes.
